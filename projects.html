Education

We believe that the purpose of education is to empower children to become self-motivated and independent learners. Islahe Millat (IM) strives to develop educational opportunities for all children based on this idea. IM conducts IEC (Information, Education, and Communication) activities to raise awareness about the importance of education through the following initiatives: Community Mobilization and Awareness Activities: Engaging with local communities to highlight the value of education and encourage participation in educational programs. School Bag & Dress Distribution to BPL Children: Providing essential school supplies and clothing to children from Below Poverty Line (BPL) families to support their educational journey and ensure they have the necessary resources to succeed. These efforts aim to bridge educational gaps and create an environment where every child has the opportunity to thrive and build a brighter future.

Agricultural Program

Farming has always been one of the most important economic activities in India. With about 2/3rd of the population engaged in agriculture and agriculture-related activities, it is important to understand the plight of farmers in India. According to a statement by the Maharashtra government in 2018, 639 Indian farmers committed suicide within 3 months due to drought and debts. Farmers need to be educated about the schemes and grants provided by the government.

Old Age Welfare

We work for old age society as we know that majority of 71% of the elderly population lives in rural India and 29% in the urban. Factors like literacy level, economic independence, age-old dependency ratio, diseases and other health impacts vary. With rapid urbanisation and a shift in socio-economic priorities, traditional family structures and values have changed. Meager old age pensions provided by the government (Rs. 200 per month in many states barring few like Goa where it is Rs. 2000) are insufficient.

Free Health Checkup & Vaccination

A Free Health Checkup & Vaccination Program aims to provide accessible healthcare services to underserved communities, particularly those without easy access to medical facilities. The initiative focuses on offering free health checkups, including screenings for common diseases such as diabetes, hypertension, and anemia, as well as providing essential vaccinations for children and adults, his program helps identify health issues early, preventing the spread of contagious diseases, and improving overall community health..
