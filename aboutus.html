Islahe Millat


About Our Organisation

Islahe Millat Trust is a non-governmental organization based in Bihar, founded in the year 2024, working on issues affecting both urban and rural populations, with a special focus on Education, Agriculture, Environment, Health, and Women Empowerment. The Islahe Millat Trust is making concerted efforts, in collaboration with the community, to improve the living conditions of the deprived sections of society. The Islahe Millat Trust team consists of individuals from diverse multidisciplinary and multi-sector backgrounds across Bihar, who share a common platform of thinking and work towards the creation of a cognitive society. Their collective efforts are aimed at uplifting the lives of those who grow up with inadequate education, healthcare, resources, and psychological support—often referred to as the "underprivileged."

A Governing Committee, consisting of 9 members elected democratically by the General Body, governs Islahe Millat Trust. Elections for the board are held every five years. The Trust operates with a fully decentralized management system, headed by the Secretary and supported by a team of professional and administrative staff. Program performance and activities are regularly monitored during quarterly review and planning meetings. Eminent experts from various fields such as management, agriculture, environment, health, women empowerment, and social work are invited to contribute to these meetings

Organisational structure

Islahe Millat Trust believes in multiplying its efforts through collaboration and partnerships that bring together government, non-government, and corporate sectors in a collaborative process for socio-economic development, improving agriculture, environment, health, and women empowerment. Islahe Millat Trust places great emphasis on upgrading the skills and capacities of its staff. To achieve this, need-based training is regularly organized, This structure ensures that the organization operates efficiently and is able to meet its social mission while complying with legal, financial, and administrative requirements. Keep in mind that while this is a general organizational model, it can vary significantly based on the size of the NGO, its scope, and specific areas of focus in Angola or any other country.

Vision

We aim to create a development revolution for the marginalized and socio-economically weaker sections of society, Education NGO Vision, "A world where every child has access to quality education, regardless of their background or geographic location." Environmental NGO Vision, "A sustainable planet where communities live in harmony with nature, preserving resources for future generations." Healthcare NGO Vision, "Universal access to healthcare services for all, ensuring that no one is left behind in the pursuit of good health." Human Rights NGO Vision, "A just and equitable world where human rights are respected, and every person lives with dignity, freedom, and equality." Poverty Alleviation NGO Vision, "A world where poverty no longer exists, and all individuals have the opportunity to live a prosperous and fulfilling life." Each vision statement reflects the core mission of the organization, aiming for a transformative and positive change in the world..

Mission

The mission of Islahe Millat NGO can be described in a few key lines, reflecting its commitment to social welfare, education, and empowerment: Empowering Communities: Islahe Millat NGO aims to uplift marginalized communities through education, healthcare, and skill development programs, fostering long-term sustainable growth. Promoting Social Justice: The organization strives to promote equality, justice, and human rights, advocating for the rights of the underprivileged and vulnerable groups in society. Facilitating Holistic Development: Islahe Millat focuses on the holistic development of individuals, emphasizing the importance of education, healthcare, and social welfare in building stronger communities. Building a Compassionate Society: The NGO is dedicated to fostering compassion, tolerance, and solidarity among people, working towards a more inclusive and harmonious society. Creating Awareness for Positive Change: Islahe Millat works to raise awareness on critical social issues, empowering individuals to take active roles in shaping a better future for all.
